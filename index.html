📱 Share App via QR Code

Generate a QR code so others can open VitalTrack on their device.

Step 1 — Embed your config into the app file

When others scan your QR code they open a fresh browser with no Firebase config saved, which is why they see the setup screen. Download a pre-configured copy of the app — your Firebase project is baked directly into the file so every visitor goes straight to login.

After downloading: rename to index.html, then drag & drop the folder on netlify.com to redeploy. Your URL stays the same.

Step 2 — Share the QR code
Enter your URL above and click Generate QR
